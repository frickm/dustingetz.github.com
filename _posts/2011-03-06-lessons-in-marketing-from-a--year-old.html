Lessons in marketing from a 15-year-old
so this guy @antimatter15 has a bunch of small open source apps, including drag2up, a nifty chrome extension for uploading files. Antimatter15 writes:

drag2up was written by @antimatter15. The source code is available on github. A totally irrelevant fact is that at time of writing I'm 15 years old, but that tends to make people treat this as more of an accomplishment than it really is and thus more inclined to share it. So now that you are aware of this, you should follow me on twitter and share this extension with everyone you know.[1]
I've read most of his stuff and if I had to guess his age I'd say he's a talented twenty-something. The above statement demonstrates acute social awareness and understanding of how to utilize it, which I find unlikely for a 15 year old guy with brilliant technical skills. OTOH, I tend to bias my perception to think people are more like me than they actually are. Whatever.

However old he is, he's utilizing[2] social tendencies for his gain, which is brilliant.

[1] This is from the chrome extension settings page, so I can't provide a citation
[2] Fluff-word lovers, take note: proper use of the word 'utilize'!! (compare to 'use')
